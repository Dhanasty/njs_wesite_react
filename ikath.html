Ikath Collection

Discover the ancient art of Ikat weaving through our exquisite collection. Each saree tells a story of precision, patience, and unparalleled artistry in tie-dye technique.

Ancient Craft

Ikat is a resist dyeing technique where threads are bound and dyed before weaving, creating unique blur-edged patterns that are truly one-of-a-kind.

Precision Artistry

Each Ikath saree requires months of meticulous planning and execution, with artisans calculating every thread placement for perfect pattern alignment.

Cultural Heritage

Our Ikath collection preserves traditional motifs and techniques passed down through generations of master weavers from Andhra Pradesh and Odisha.

The Ikath Process

1

Design Planning

Master weavers create detailed patterns and calculate thread requirements

2

Thread Binding

Threads are bound tightly with resist materials in predetermined patterns

3

Natural Dyeing

Bound threads are dyed with natural colors, creating unique patterns

4

Precision Weaving

Dyed threads are carefully woven to align patterns perfectly

Understanding Ikath Patterns

Geometric Motifs

Traditional diamonds, triangles, and zigzag patterns that represent cosmic elements and natural phenomena.

Floral Designs

Stylized flowers and leaves that showcase the artisan's skill in creating organic forms through the resist-dye process.

Cultural Symbols

Sacred motifs and regional symbols that carry deep cultural significance and tell stories of heritage.

Modern Fusion

Contemporary interpretations that blend traditional techniques with modern aesthetics for today's fashion.
